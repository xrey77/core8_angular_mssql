About Us

Barclays is a major British universal bank with roots dating back to 1690, offering retail, corporate, wealth management, and investment banking services globally, known for its UK-centered focus, vast history, and operations in over 40 countries, with a prominent presence in the US consumer market and a diversified structure encompassing UK Banking and Barclays International.

Structure & Services:
Operates through five divisions: UK Consumer Banking, UK Corporate Banking, Wealth & Private Banking, Barclays Corporate & Investment Bank (CIB), and a specialist US Consumer Bank, providing everything from current accounts and mortgages to complex investment banking.
